Frequently Asked Questions

What is a hotel booking engine?

A hotel booking engine is a tool embedded on your hotel's own website that lets guests check availability and book rooms directly, without going through an OTA like Booking.com or MakeMyTrip.

Does Billzify's booking engine charge commission?

No. Direct bookings made through Billzify's booking engine carry zero OTA commission, so you keep 100% of your direct booking revenue.

Does it sync with my PMS and channel manager?

Yes. The booking engine syncs in real time with Billzify PMS and Channel Manager, so rates and availability shown to visitors are always accurate.

Can I add it to my existing hotel website?

Yes. Billzify's booking engine is designed for easy integration with your existing website and can be customized to match your hotel's branding.

Is the booking engine mobile-friendly?

Yes. Since over 70% of travelers book on mobile, Billzify's booking engine is built mobile-first and works smoothly on phones, tablets, and desktops.

Is the booking engine included with Billzify PMS?

Yes, the booking engine works alongside Billzify PMS as part of your setup. Get in touch for full details on what's included.
