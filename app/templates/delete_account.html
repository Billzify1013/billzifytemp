Account Deletion Request

To request deletion of your Billzify account and associated data, please email us at info@billzify.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" along with your registered username/email.

We will process your request within 30 days. The following data will be deleted upon request:
- Personal information (name, email, phone number)
- Booking history
- Account credentials

Some data may be retained for legal/accounting compliance purposes (such as transaction records) as required by applicable law.

For any queries, please contact us at info@billzify.com
