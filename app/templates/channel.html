Frequently Asked Questions

What is a hotel channel manager?

A channel manager lets you update room rates and availability across multiple OTAs — like Booking.com, MakeMyTrip, Airbnb, and Expedia — from a single dashboard, instead of updating each one manually.

How much does a channel manager cost in India?

Billzify's channel manager starts at just ₹1 per room per day, with no hidden setup fees — making it one of the most affordable options for Indian hotels.

Which OTAs does Billzify connect with?

Billzify connects with 100+ OTAs and metasearch platforms, including Booking.com, MakeMyTrip, Airbnb, Agoda, Expedia, Goibibo, Google Hotels, and TripAdvisor.

Does a channel manager prevent double bookings?

Yes. Billzify syncs inventory in real time across every connected OTA — once a room is booked anywhere, it's instantly blocked everywhere else, eliminating overbookings.

Is Billzify suitable for small hotels?

Yes. Billzify is built for hotels, resorts, homestays, and guesthouses of every size across India, with simple onboarding and no technical expertise required.

Do I need technical knowledge to set it up?

No. Billzify's team handles onboarding and OTA connections for you, and the dashboard is designed to be simple enough for any hotel staff member to use.
